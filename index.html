Learn to Code with Scratch

Scratch is designed by MIT to teach kids, ages 8 to 16, computer programming. Millions of students are creating Scratch projects in a wide variety of settings, including homes, schools, museums, libraries, and community centers.

Computer Science Has Many Opportunities

Código Academy focuses student’s creativity and curiosity through hands-on robotics projects and software labs. This forms the foundation needed at an early age to continue learning about technology and guiding kids to a career in technology.
